About me

My name is Raimon Hubbard but I go by Ray! Currently, I study software devlopement at devCodeCamp! I'm originally from Michigan, but as of this year I'm based out of Little Rock Arkansas. I first became interested in IT during my time in the Navy. When I got out, I decided to take classes but I quickly lost interest and got a job at the US Postal Service. I worked there for a year and made good money but... there was still something missing.....One day I was bored and looking to download games. I made a mistake and downloaded Sololearn. Not knowing what it was, I opened the app and 5 minutes later I printed "Hello World" for the first time. Ive been in love ever since! From there I set out looking for more information about the field. After some thought I decided to change my career! Though I knew the journey wouldnt be easy, I admit I underestimated how dificult it would be to learn this craft. After trying to learn on my own for a little less than a year I kept running into concepts I couldn't understand for the life of me! That how I becaeme a student at devCodeCamp! Its been one of, if not the most, challenging courses I've ever taken but fun at the same time! So far we have covered javascript and html5. Learning Javascript was challenging! Its literally like learning a new language. Any slip of the tongue and you wont be able to get your message across the way you intended. My first project will show a basic trip randomizer. Using functions and conditional statements I created an application that randomly selects a trip for the user. To see more of my projects visit the PROJECTS section of this site... Speaking of site.. this website youre viewing was created by me as my second project! I made it using html5 and css. How does it look? Any feedback is well appreciated!

As mentioned in the about me section, I am also a veteran of the US Navy! I was stationed in Norfolk, Va onboard the aircraft carrier USS Harry S Truman. I served onboard the flight deck as an Aircraft Director. During my enlistment I deployed 2 times with the USS Harry S Truman and I volunteered to deploy with the USS George Bush. I recieved the Good Conduct ribbon and two recomendations from the Commander of my fleet. I was honorably discharged in 2017.
